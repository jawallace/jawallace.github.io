My name is Jeff Wallace, and I am a senior at Northeastern University studying toward a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Physics, with a minor in Mathematics. Through Northeastern's co-op program, I have gained approximately 14 months of full-time experience working on a team of software developers in an agile environment. During these work experiences I have worked on both production and prototype systems utilizing a variety of technologies.

My first co-op was at Basis Technologies, Inc in Cambridge, Massachussetts where I worked on Autopsy, an open-source digital-forensics application built on top of the popular SleuthKit forensics framework.
My accomplishments at Basis included:

  • contributing bug fixes
  • improving the testing framework
  • implementing a CI system
  • improving the UI
  • overhauling and extending the email ingest module

As part of a team of scientists and developers, I worked on a prototype web application utilizing semantic web technology to streamline the development of training programs for a government customer. I joined the team early in the engineering process and played a critical role in the major design and development tasks.
